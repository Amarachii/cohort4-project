About Me

As aforementioned, my name is Agbasielo Jacinta Amarachi and I am a junior front-end web developer for now and also a face-model. I also have good interest in content writing.

A word to describe Amarachi is a go-getter. I am a versatile person, always open to learn, unlearn and just be better at whatever I do. I am a strong aspirant for opportunities to advance.

I am a student of Nnamdi Azikiwe University [UNIZIK]. I ventured into tech in search of a skill, just a skill to have. At the beginning I did not have a solid reason for choosing web development but I am glad I did. I know I have a lot to offer in this track.

A major strain in my coding learning is exhaustion because honestly it is not easy but it is definitely worth the work. I have learnt some things so far and two very important things I know that guide me now are: Learning is a journey not a destination and also good time management is very important.

One of my goals is to create a sturdy career for myself in tech soon enough and most importantly enjoy and plough through every part of journey.

This is just a little about Amarachi.
